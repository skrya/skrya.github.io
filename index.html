I am a PhD student advised by Junsong Yuan in Computer Science from University at Buffalo. \textbf{My research focuses on designing video perception and forecasting models for autonomous systems that will enable autonomous systems to \textit{perceive} and \textit{forecast} in the real world just as humans are able, and ultimately surpass humans ability to forecast.} In Video Perception: I developed efficient and domain adaptable models that enable autonomous agents to understand scene semantics from images and videos. In Future Forecasting, Leveraging video perception to design models with enhanced abilities to predict future scenes, making use of depth information to encode 3D knowledge of the world. In \textbf{Multimodal Future Forecasting}, Integrate vision, agent intentions as language, to create models that achieve far greater forecasting controllability than visual input alone.

In grad school, I've had the opportunity to intern at Amazon (with Tian Lan). Before that, I worked at Adobe. I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from IIIT Hyderabad in 2015. In my free time, I enjoy reading, running, and cricket.
